The Company

Rosie Home Tech is a fast-growing consumer robotics company headquartered in Raleigh, North Carolina. Their flagship product line — the Rosie robot series — represents the next generation of AI-powered home assistants. Three models are currently on the market:

ModelFeaturesPrice
Rosie ClassicCleaning, cooking, and laundry$899

Rosie Home Tech sells through 47 retail partner locations across the country. Sales associates at these locations are employed by the retail partners but complete onboarding through Rosie Home Tech's training portal when they begin selling the product line.

The Performance Problem

Six months after the national launch, Rosie Home Tech's regional sales managers are raising urgent concerns. Despite strong foot traffic and high product awareness among customers, conversion rates are 41% below the projected target. Customers are picking up the display units, engaging with associates, and then leaving without purchasing.

Regional managers are reporting that customers are leaving with unresolved questions — particularly around AI privacy, product reliability, smart home compatibility, and price justification. The source and scale of these concerns has not been formally documented.

Regional managers also report that associates seem knowledgeable about hardware specs but struggle when conversations shift to AI behavior, personalization features, and value comparison across the three models. Several managers have noted that associates are "reading from the card" rather than having genuine conversations with customers.

Rosie Home Tech's VP of Sales has engaged your instructional design team to investigate and recommend a training intervention. You have not yet collected any data. That is your first task.

Your role: You are the instructional designer on this project. Before you can analyze anything, you must decide how you will gather information about the performance gap.
